Roadmap

What we build next

This roadmap follows the research: web-first shareable games now, stronger templates next, and mobile export only after the builder is stable. No fake parity claims. No hidden scope.

Now

Stabilize the builder

  • Keep landing, workspace, gallery, and team pages consistent.
  • Keep manifest-driven game listing working without relying on a broken API.
  • Make the preview loop reliable before adding more systems.
Next

Strengthen game templates

  • Improve the Phaser brawler path.
  • Keep Three.js world generation valid and playable.
  • Add better starter prompts for combat, zones, leveling, and loot.
Then

Make the agents visible

  • Show the role split clearly on the Team page.
  • Keep planning, building, and validation separated.
  • Use parallel agents for roadmap and implementation work.
Later

Mobile export parity

  • Expo / RN export is deferred.
  • Only build it after the web builder is solid.
  • Use it as a parity path, not the core product.

Research alignment: Rork is mobile-app-first. ForgeLoop is game-first, web-first, and shareable by default.

Roadmap stays public. Scope stays small enough to ship.
